Vitality

Team Vitality is a French professional esports organisation founded in August 2013 by Fabien Devide and Nicolas Maurer. It has several teams competing in top-level leagues across Europe and India. Team Vitality's main League of Legends team competes in the LEC (formerly EU LCS), which they joined in late 2015 after purchasing Gambit Gaming's spot.
